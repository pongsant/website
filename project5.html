05

Objective: Create a **data portrait** based on a personal habit.

For this assignment, I tracked how often I listen to music and what types of music I enjoy. Over a week, I collected data on the genres I listened to** and how frequently I played them.

Process:

  • Habit Tracked: I recorded how many times I listened to different music genres (e.g., pop, rock, classical).
  • Data Collection: Each time I listened to music, I wrote down the **genre and frequency**.
  • Visualization: I transformed this data into an **animated self-portrait using p5.js**:
    • Colors: Each genre is represented by a different color (e.g., blue for pop, red for rock, green for classical).
    • Size: The larger the shape, the more I listened to that genre.
    • Animation: Shapes **move or pulse** to represent the **rhythm and energy** of my music habits.

How to Read the Data Portrait:
- Colors represent different music genres.
- Shape size shows how frequently I listened to each genre.
- Animation adds movement to reflect the music’s rhythm.

This project helped me **turn my music-listening habits into an interactive and artistic experience**.
