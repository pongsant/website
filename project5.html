Project 5

Objective: Make a data portrait based on one of your personal habits. For Assignment 6, I tracked how often I listen to music and the types of music I enjoy. Over a week, I collected data on the genres I listened to and how often I played them. Using this data, I created a self-portrait in p5.js with animation. In the portrait, each color represents a music genre, and the size of the shapes shows how much I listened to that genre. Process: 1. Habit Tracked: I tracked how many times I listened to different types of music (like pop, rock, or classical) for a few days. 2. Data Collection: Each time I listened to music, I wrote down the genre and how often I played it, creating a numbered list. 3. Visualization: I turned this data into an animated portrait using p5.js: - Colors: Each color stands for a different music genre (e.g., blue for pop, red for rock, green for classical). - Size: Bigger shapes mean I listened to that genre more often. - Animation: The shapes move or pulse, showing the rhythm and energy of my music habits. How to Read the Data Portrait: - The colors show the type of music I listened to. - The size of the shapes shows how much I listened to that genre. - The animation represents the feeling and rhythm of the music. This project helped me turn my music-listening habits into a fun and creative artwork.
