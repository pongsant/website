About Me

Hi, I'm Pongsant Chintanapakdee, a 21-year-old student at Parsons School of Design in New York City. I specialize in Design and Technology, blending artistic creativity with technical innovation. My work explores the intersections of culture, technology, and personal expression. I aim to create impactful designs that inspire and connect with people worldwide.
