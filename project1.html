Project 1: Apple Pencil in p5.js

This assignment explores how memories and descriptions are interpreted differently when translated into visuals through programming.

My partner described an **Apple Pencil**, and I took on the challenge of coding its representation in **p5.js**. This was my first time using p5.js, so I had to break complex tasks into small steps.
