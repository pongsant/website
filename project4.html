04

Objective: Create an animated optical illusion that hides information or creates interactive patterns.

This week’s lecture focused on how optical illusions can be used to hide messages, personal details, or locations** within patterns. Inspired by this, I designed a moving dot illusion that reacts to user interaction.

My Design:

  • Pattern: The design consists of dots in three colors: black, grey, and white.
  • Interaction: Clicking on a dot triggers a wave-like motion.
  • Stop Motion: Clicking the dot again pauses the movement.
  • Unique Movement: Each dot follows its **own independent motion**, creating an engaging effect.

This project highlights how optical illusions can make patterns visually dynamic and engaging. By integrating user interaction, I aimed to create a personalized and immersive** experience. Each dot behaves differently, making the entire composition feel alive.
