Project 3

Objective: Create a creative and unique clock that tracks time in a new way. Instead of using the usual hours, minutes, and seconds like a regular clock, this project focuses on showing time differently. The goal is to make a clock that is more personal, artistic, or abstract. For this project, I came up with a clock inspired by the human eye. It has three layers of colors, and each one shows a different unit of time: - White Layer: Shows the hours. - Grey Layer: Shows the minutes. - Black Layer: Shows the seconds. The layers change over time, just like an eye might shift and move. This clock doesnâ€™t just show the time; it also looks artistic and creative as it changes. I used simple coding techniques like `if/else` statements to make the layers change. Functions like `millis()`, `second()`, `minute()`, and `hour()` track the time, and the `map()` function helps turn those time values into movements and changes in the clock. This project creates a fun and different way to think about and see time!
